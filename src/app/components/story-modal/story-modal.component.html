Howdy! We are the Story Next Door team We focus our project around tackeling inequalities, by gathering stories about real people.

After that we are going to translate them into the language of kids, in the hopes that we can help them become more aware towards these real life social problems.

What if an individual tells you that it’s NOT okay to have freckles on your face, just because someone told them so.

What if you live in a country where they forbid people of the same sex to be married to one another, just because someone told them so.

It’s fine to have an opinion but it is also fine if someone else does not share that opinion. And that’s why we want to challenge the taboos around them

The gathered stories will be presented as a fun, interactive and adventurous book for children to explore.

This way they can really connect themselves to the story in the best way possible, PLAYING!
